Operational Workflow

Three phases.
One agentic pipeline.

Every command triggers a structured 3-phase workflow designed to decompose, orchestrate, and critically refine your creative output.

Phase 01
🧩
Intent Decomposition
Your prompt is analyzed to identify the goal type, extract constraints, and break the task into an ordered chain of sub-tasks.
Detect: new creation / edit / series
Extract dimensions, hex colors, style DNA
Build ordered sub-task chain
Phase 02
🎛️
Model Orchestration
The orchestrator selects the optimal ML model for each sub-task — from diffusion models to ControlNet, CLIP, and upscalers.
Diffusion models for base imagery
ControlNet for structural integrity
LLM/VLM for caption & metadata
Upscaler for detail injection
Phase 03
🔍
Critic Loop
A simulated Critic Agent scores the output 1–10. If the score falls below 8.0, a hidden Refine step is automatically triggered.
Anatomy & artifact check
Intent alignment scoring
Auto-refine if score < 8/10
